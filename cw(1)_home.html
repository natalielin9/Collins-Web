Collins Web

Collins Web is a site containing the youth friendly places and prices in Fort Collins, CO.

These places are spaces outside of school, home, and work for young peopleto hang out, explore opportunities, and engage with their community. These businesses and organizations will offer youth-friendly promotions, hours, events, and environments. Collins Web will be launched on a free phone app (along with this website) and will enhance youth belongingness and connections with their peers and community.

This site was created by Youth Action for Health which is a youth-led coalition in Fort Collins working to help youth mental health in our community.

  • How to Use Collins Web

    Use the menu at the top underneath the title to get to the various pages. In the events page you will see what events are going on. Here, you can see a list of events with their details on time, location, and price.

  • How to Use Public Transportation

    Here in Fort Collins we have a public transporation system. We have buses that reach out and then the MaxLine which goes north south.

  • What To Do When You Get To The Event

    If there is a code, when you are in person let the employee know that you have a Youth Price code. You can remember the code from the site or pull up the site and show the employee.
